⭐ Top Highlights of Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is one of the most diverse travel destinations in Asia. Here are some highlights:

  • 🏛️ Ancient cities like Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa
  • 🌄 Scenic hill country in Ella and Nuwara Eliya
  • 🏖️ Beautiful beaches in Mirissa and Unawatuna
  • 🐘 Wildlife safaris in Yala and Udawalawe National Parks
  • 🍛 Delicious Sri Lankan cuisine and spices

🧭 Travel Information

Planning your trip is easy with the right information. Sri Lanka offers a tropical climate, friendly locals, and a wide range of travel options.

  • 🌤️ Best time to visit: December – April (West & South)
  • 💱 Currency: Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR)
  • 🛂 Visa: ETA required for most travelers
  • 🚆 Transport: Trains, buses, and private taxis available
