Sigiriya

Sigiriya is one of the most famous and travelled-to destinations in Sri Lanka.

Ella

Ella may seem like just another small town set against Sri Lanka’s rolling countryside, but look a little closer and you’ll find one of the best locations to visit and stay in the whole island!

Galle

Home to a UNESCO World Heritage Site, Galle is a must-visit destination during your holiday in Sri Lanka.

Mirissa

Found on the southern coast of Sri Lanka, Mirissa is a seemingly small town found by the coast.

Kandy

Home to a myriad of historic landmarks, iconic temples and botanical spaces, Kandy is the second-largest city in the country.

Anuradhapura

Renowned as the cultural heard of the country, Anuradhapura is Sri Lanka’s first major capital lined with age-old temples.

Colombo

The cosmopolitan metropolis city of Colombo, Sri Lanka is a tropical sanctuary with a rich colonial heritage.

Dambulla

A historic coastal city located on the central plains of Sri Lanka, Dambulla Cave Temple is renowned for its ancient rock cave complex featuring sacred Buddhist statues and centuries-old mural paintings.

Trincomalee

A port city situated on the northeast coast of Sri Lanka, Trincomalee has a recorded history of more than two and a half thousand years.

Yala

Sri Lanka is considered to be one of the best locations in Asia to go on safari.
